Internal Privacy Policy

Data handling guidelines for administrators.

Data Collection & Analytics

The EliTechWiz internal dashboard logs administrator actions (IP address, timestamp, modifications made) strictly for security auditing and accountability purposes. This data is not shared externally.

Authentication Tokens

We use secure, short-lived tokens and cookies to manage your session. By logging into the portal, you consent to the storage of these necessary technical cookies.

Client Identifiable Information (CII)

Administrators with access to project details or client contact pages must handle all data in compliance with internal security guidelines. Do not export raw client JSON data onto unprotected devices.
